About Me

I'm no tech guru, I'm a nerd who loves to learn all that is security but also a wide veriety of different subjects. My goal is to put what I learn to good use and make a difference in the security landscape of people around me and especially the not-so-tech-savvy majority of people. I also bring many years of customer service experience, which taught me the value of people care and clear communication. For the past 2+ years, I’ve been working in the security field, and before that I gained valuable experience in the Fraud Department at Desjardins Bank, where I developed a sharp eye for threats and risks. I’m always looking to connect with others who have questions preferably security-related or about the projects I’ve posted here, but I welcome any topic. I might not always have the answer right away, but I’ll either find it or work through it together with you. And even if you don’t have a question, I’d love for you to reach out through the contacts at the bottom of this page. I know I have much to learn from the experiences and insights of others.
