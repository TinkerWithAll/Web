Who am I?

My name is Zachary Baillod,
and I am a cybersecurity enthusiast and Junior Analyst passionate about solving problems with creative and effective solutions. With a background in technical roles and a knack for automation, I thrive on challenges that allow me to learn and innovate. Based in Montreal, I am pursuing a dynamic career in the cybersecurity domain. My journey includes experiences ranging from fraud detection and red team operations to test the security controls. Whether identifying malicious activity in Azure Sentinel or automating workflows to save valuable time, I bring curiosity, precision, and adaptability to every task. In my spare time, I often get carried away exploring and understanding vulnerabilities in the systems I interact with daily. This relentless curiosity drives me to uncover potential weaknesses and deepen my understanding of how to secure them. With aspirations to achieve certifications like the CompTIA Security+ and OSCP, I am eager to grow, share my knowledge, and contribute to a team driven by curiosity and a commitment to security excellence.
